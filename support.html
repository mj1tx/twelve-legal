Common Questions

Why are my prayer times slightly off?

Prayer times depend on your location and calculation method. Try switching the method in Settings, or use Manual Offsets to fine-tune individual times.

How do I change my location?

Go to Settings → Location. You can search for any city or tap "Use current location".

I'm not receiving notifications

Make sure notifications are enabled in Settings → Notifications, and that Twelve has permission in your iPhone's Settings → Notifications → Twelve.

What is the Catch-Up Queue?

Any prayer you mark as Missed is added to your catch-up queue. When you complete it later, mark it as Qada to clear it from the list.

How do I reset my prayer log?

Deleting and reinstalling the app will clear all data. A reset option will be added in a future update.
