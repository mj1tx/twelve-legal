Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026
Short version: Ahd does not collect, share, or sell any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Who we are

Ahd is a prayer time app built for the UK Shia Muslim community. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at support@twelveapps.xyz.

2. Data we collect

We collect no data. Specifically:

  • No account required. You do not sign up or log in.
  • No analytics. We do not use any analytics, tracking, or crash-reporting services.
  • No advertising. We do not show ads and have no ad networks integrated.
  • No server. The app has no backend. Nothing you do in the app is transmitted anywhere.

3. Data stored on your device

The following information is stored locally on your device only. It never leaves your device:

  • Your name — used to personalise the greeting.
  • Your location — city name, coordinates, and timezone — used to calculate prayer times.
  • Prayer log — your record of prayed/missed prayers.
  • App preferences — notification settings, calculation method, and time offsets.

This data is stored in AsyncStorage on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all of it.

4. Location

If you grant location permission, the app reads your device's GPS coordinates once to determine your city and timezone. These coordinates are stored locally and used only to calculate prayer times. They are never transmitted to any server.

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings under Location Services → Twelve (iOS) or App Permissions → Location → Twelve (Android). You can also set your location manually by searching for a city.

5. Notifications

If you enable prayer reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. No notification content is sent through any external service. All notifications are generated and delivered entirely on-device.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings under Notifications → Twelve (iOS) or App Notifications → Twelve (Android).

6. Third-party services

Ahd uses no third-party SDKs that collect data. The app is built on Expo and React Native, which are open-source frameworks that do not independently collect user data.

7. Children's privacy

Ahd does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, there is no special risk to younger users.

8. Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy — for example, if we add any data collection in a future version — we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. We will notify users through an in-app notice for any material changes.

9. Contact

Questions or concerns? Email us at support@twelveapps.xyz.
